Members of the lab attended the
[**International Conference on Flow Dynamics 2025 (ICFD 2025)**](https://www.ifs.tohoku.ac.jp/icfd/2025/)
on **November 10–13, 2025**. The conference is organized by the
**Institute of Fluid Science, Tohoku University**, and was held at the
**Sendai International Center** (Sendai, Miyagi Prefecture).

**Four undergraduate research students** received airfare support from the
**Mechanical Engineering Alumni Association** to present their preliminary
research results.
