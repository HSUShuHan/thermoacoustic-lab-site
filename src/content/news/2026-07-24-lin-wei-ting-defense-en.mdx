Master's student **Wei-Ting Lin** passed his thesis defense on **24 July 2026**. Congratulations on completing the M.S. degree.

His thesis, **"Investigation of the Effects of Steam Injection on the Steady-State Amplitude Gain and Stability of a Standing-Wave Thermoacoustic Engine,"** studies an actively wet-steam-injected thermoacoustic engine, examining how steam injection affects the steady-state amplitude gain and stability of a standing-wave thermoacoustic engine, and identifying its limit-cycle oscillations with nonlinear-dynamics methods. The results have also been written up as a journal manuscript, currently under review.

We thank the thesis committee for serving as examiners: Prof. Yu-Hsi Huang (Dept. of Mechanical Engineering, National Taiwan University), Prof. Yu-Ren Wu (Dept. of Mechanical Engineering, National Central University), Prof. Han-Hsun Yang (Dept. of Aeronautics and Astronautics, National Cheng Kung University), and Prof. Jhen-Syuan Huang (Dept. of Vehicle Engineering, National Taipei University of Technology).
